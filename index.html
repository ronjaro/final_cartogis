The data

h2>

This portfolio presents data on birds in the Turdus genus in Finland, concentrating in the summer season. The data is by Finnish Biodiversity Information Facility/FinBIF.

Link to the dataset used: http://tun.fi/HBF.107404 (accessed 27.6.2025).

Data filters used:

Target (taxon): Turdus (MX.33090)

Time: 2015-01-01/2025-01-01

Season: 0601/0831 (June-August)

Information source: Bird ringing and recovery database (TIPU)
